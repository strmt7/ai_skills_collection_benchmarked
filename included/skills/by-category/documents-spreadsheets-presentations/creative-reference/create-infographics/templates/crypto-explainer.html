Intent-Based Architecture
Users sign a declaration of intent (what they want) rather than an on-chain transaction. Off-chain solvers compete to fulfill it at the best price — separating expression from execution.
Solver Network
Competing professional market makers analyze 50+ liquidity sources in real time. The solver who delivers best execution wins the order — self-reinforcing quality guarantee.
Split Routing
Large orders are automatically split across multiple pools to minimize price impact. A $1M USDC→ETH swap may execute across 8 pools simultaneously.
Batch Settlement
Multiple user trades are batched into a single on-chain transaction. This reduces gas costs by up to 70% and enables the ~400ms settlement time.
