▼ ELIGIBILITY RULES
WHAT QUALIFIES / WHAT DISQUALIFIES
Qualifying Activities
  • Protocol Usage — any transaction on mainnet before snapshot block
  • Liquidity Provision — LP positions held for 7+ days in qualifying pools
  • Governance Voting — participated in ≥1 on-chain governance vote
  • OG Holder — held {{TICKER}} or NFT before {{EARLY_CUTOFF}}
  • Beta Participation — used testnet or closed beta program
  • Referral Activity — referred ≥3 active wallets to the platform
Disqualifying Criteria
  • Sybil Wallets — cluster analysis of linked addresses will remove farms
  • CEX Addresses — exchange hot/cold wallets are excluded from distribution
  • No Genuine Activity — wallets with zero qualifying on-chain interaction
  • Sanctioned Jurisdictions — OFAC-screened wallets auto-excluded
  • Team / Investor Wallets — addresses in team or VC allocations are excluded
  • Post-Snapshot Transfers — moving assets after block #{{SNAPSHOT_BLOCK}} has no effect
