How to Stake
  • Step 1 — Connect wallet to {{STAKING_URL}} and approve {{TICKER}} spend
  • Step 2 — Choose stake amount and lock duration (flexible or 1–4 year)
  • Step 3 — Confirm transaction — receive ve{{TICKER}} representing your locked position
  • Step 4 — Claim rewards weekly from the "Rewards" tab — auto-compound available
  • Unstaking — Initiate unbonding → wait {{UNBOND_DAYS}} days → withdraw full principal
  • Early Exit — Break lock early with {{EARLY_EXIT_FEE}}% penalty redistributed to stakers
