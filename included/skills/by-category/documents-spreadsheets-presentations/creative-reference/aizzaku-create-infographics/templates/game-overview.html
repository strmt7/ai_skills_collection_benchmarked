HOW TO PLAY

Getting Started
  • Mint or buy at least 1 character NFT from Magic Eden
  • Connect Solana wallet at arena.game/play
  • Choose a guild or create your own (5 members min)
  • Complete tutorial quest to unlock all game modes
  • Stake your NFT to begin earning daily $ARENA yield
  • Enter ranked arena battles to earn season points
Earning Mechanics
  • Daily yield: 10–120 $ARENA based on NFT rarity tier
  • Battle wins: +50% bonus yield for the battle session
  • Territory control: passive $ARENA flow to guild treasury
  • Crafting recipes burn materials → reduce supply
  • Season leaderboard top 100 → exclusive NFT airdrops
  • Referral: earn 5% of referred wallet's first-month yield
